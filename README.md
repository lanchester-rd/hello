## About Me

I am a UK-born product designer and founder based in Amsterdam.

My background is in Technology, Art & Design, with a Master of Design from Coventry University. I have worked across industrial design, IoT products, UX research, interface design, web development, product strategy, and early-stage startup building.

Today, my work is focused on building useful, human-centred software through **Lanchester Research & Design**.

I design and develop products that combine clear user experience, practical technology, and strong visual direction. My current work spans Apple-native apps, Firebase-backed platforms, AI-assisted workflows, co-parenting tools, product launch systems, and hardware-aware mobile experiences.

## Current Work

### Lanchester Research & Design

Lanchester Research & Design is my product studio for building and testing new software products.

The studio focuses on:

- Apple-native iOS, iPadOS, macOS, and tvOS apps
- UX-led digital products
- Firebase and CloudKit-backed platforms
- AI-assisted product workflows
- Product launch systems
- Human-centred tools for families, creators, students, teams, and communities

### iMediate

iMediate is a co-parenting product designed to help separated parents manage calendars, communication, parenting plans, documentation, and family logistics with more clarity and less conflict.

### LEARN

LEARN, the Lanchester Early Access Research Network, is a platform for product discovery, audience building, early access, partner workflows, and launch operations across the Lanchester product portfolio.
