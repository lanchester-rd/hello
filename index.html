introduction

Thomas' range of design expertises provide him with all sorts of methods to work with and combine in new ways to solve your research, design or growth problem.

His imaginative thinking skills provide Thomas with the ability to see things in new ways, recognise behaviour patterns and make connections across products to enhance your customer's experience.

Embracing his venturesome personality, that contributes heavily to his creativity, he routinely seeks new experiences, takes calcualted risks and is an experienced sailor.

How do you partner with teams across a company?

I find the best way is to ensure that all goals are aligned to begin with; if everyone is getting something from the project, the higher the output.

Look across teams for members who have a good rapport and capitalise on their relationships by encouraging communication and updates between teams.

Because I wanted to make the world a better place and I figured starting with the interfaces we use everyday was a good start.

PumpWatch

preparing petrol stations for the future

I joined security company BigBrother tasked with designing a large scale Web App with a complex feature set, additionaly to introduce Agile/UX processes at a company with €1 Million turnover.

The final interface is made of 100+ screens that combine transaction information with the correct video footage, presenting alongside additional information such as; the usage of the Carwash, number of visits over the last three months and vehicle information tracked via licence plate.

Throughout the year I worked closely with the directors and select customers to design the interface, the product launched in November 2018 to a huge comerical success and is the most popular security camera platform for Petrol Stations in the Benelux region.
